# Change Log

## 1.2.0 — Dojo profile, automatic term calendar and student billing

- Expanded Settings with complete dojo identity, contact, address, venue and invoice details.
- Added editable weekly class days and venue address.
- Added Term 1–4 date fields in Settings for the selected academic year.
- Saving term dates now synchronises normal training sessions automatically.
- Sessions created from the term calendar appear in Attendance without separate entry.
- Changing term dates, normal training days, times or venue updates future sessions without attendance history.
- Sessions outside the revised term calendar are safely cancelled when they have no attendance history.
- Billable payment weeks are recalculated from distinct Monday–Sunday weeks containing a scheduled or completed normal class.
- Manual session cancellation or restoration recalculates term payment weeks.
- Added fee defaults in Settings: $20 weekly, $120 first family member term fee and $100 additional family member term fee.
- Added editable referral rules matching the dojo programme.
- Added Student Hub fields for family fee position, custom amount, custom amount period, qualifying referrals, reward terms remaining and permanent free training.
- Added an automatic fee preview to Student Hub.
- Student billing settings are stored once and used by Fees & Ledgers.
- Weekly charges can calculate all billable weeks for a selected term.
- Term charges automatically apply first-member or sibling pricing, custom student amounts and available referral rewards.
- Referral rewards are consumed when used on a term charge, except permanent rewards.
- Student creation now links the student to the current term enrolment automatically.
- Attendance now selects the nearest upcoming session and respects the student start and leaving dates.
- Receipts and invoices use the expanded dojo contact details.
- Preserved safe family deletion and all Student Hub linking from version 1.1.1.

## 1.1.1 — Safe family deletion

## 1.1.0 — Linked Student Hub
