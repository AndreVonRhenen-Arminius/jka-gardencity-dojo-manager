# Student Hub Guide

## Default add and edit section

Use **Student Hub** for normal additions and edits. It is the source of truth for
student, family, billing and primary-guardian information.

## Adding a student

1. Open **Student Hub**.
2. Select **Add student & family**.
3. Complete the student section.
4. Select an existing family or create one in the same form.
5. Select an existing guardian or create the primary guardian in the same form.
6. Confirm consent decisions.
7. Save the linked student.

The saved student then appears automatically in:

- Attendance
- Gradings
- Progress & Goals
- Fees & Ledgers
- Payments & Invoices
- Reports
- Communication

## Editing information

Select **Edit master record** beside the student. A change to the family payment
reference or guardian email updates the shared record used by the other modules.

## Student-specific protected information

Select **Profile & missing info** for:

- emergency contacts
- protected medical information
- student notes
- safety alerts
- missing-information email drafts

## Families & Guardians

This page is now primarily a linked directory. Use its advanced actions only for:

- correcting shared family address or billing information
- adding an additional guardian

## Missing-information email

The profile shows a completeness percentage and lists missing or unconfirmed items.
It creates a personalised parent or guardian email. Use:

- **Copy email draft** to paste it into Gmail or another service
- **Open in email app** to create a pre-addressed email
- **Log request & follow-up** to record the request and create a task due in seven days
