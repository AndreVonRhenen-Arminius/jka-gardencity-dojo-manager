# Term and Student Billing Guide

## Term calendar

Enter Term 1–4 dates in Settings and save. The app creates normal sessions on the configured training days. Attendance reads those session records directly.

A payment week is a Monday–Sunday week containing at least one normal session that is scheduled or completed. Cancelling every normal session in a week reduces the term payment-week count.

## Student amount

Open Student Hub and edit the student master record. The billing section contains:

- payment plan
- automatic first/additional family member position
- optional family-position override
- optional custom amount
- custom amount period: weekly or term
- qualifying referral count
- referral discount
- reward terms remaining
- permanent free-training status

A custom amount overrides the normal term, sibling and referral calculation.

## Charge calculation

In Fees & Ledgers:

- a weekly student can be charged for one week or all payment weeks in the selected term;
- a term student automatically uses the first-member or sibling amount;
- available referral rewards are applied to term training fees;
- gasshuku and grading charges are not reduced by referral rewards;
- the original amount, discount and final amount are stored separately.
